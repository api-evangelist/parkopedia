S&P Global Mobility spoke to Hans-Hendrik Puvogel, Parkopedia's Chief Operating Officer, to understand the in-car payments space as it stands today and how it is likely to evolve going forward. In a wide-ranging chat with S&P Global Mobility's Vivek Beriwal, Puvogel expands on Parkopedia’s journey and highlights the significance of its in-house developed payment processing platform, which has become integral to the Parkopedia solution and its value proposition for B2B customers.
